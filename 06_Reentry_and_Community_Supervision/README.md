# 06 - Reentry and Community Supervision

## Purpose
This section examines the transition from incarceration back to the community, including parole, probation, collateral consequences, and support services that facilitate successful reintegration.

## Key Topics

### Parole and Probation
Two forms of community supervision:
- **Parole**: Supervised release from prison before sentence completion
- **Probation**: Court-ordered supervision instead of incarceration
- **Conditions**: Drug testing, employment, treatment, no contact orders
- **Violations**: Technical violations vs. new crimes
- **Revocation**: Return to incarceration for violations

### Collateral Consequences
Legal and social barriers to reintegration:
- **Employment**: 70%+ of employers conduct background checks
- **Housing**: Public housing bans, landlord discrimination
- **Education**: Financial aid restrictions for drug convictions
- **Voting**: Felony disenfranchisement in many states
- **Family**: Custody restrictions, adoption barriers
- **Benefits**: Restrictions on SNAP, TANF, SSI

### Reentry Challenges
Obstacles to successful reintegration:
- **Employment**: Criminal records, gaps in work history, lack of skills
- **Housing**: Homelessness, unstable housing, geographic restrictions
- **Healthcare**: Loss of Medicaid, medication access, treatment continuity
- **Family**: Strained relationships, custody issues, financial obligations
- **Stigma**: Social isolation, discrimination, identity challenges

### Successful Reentry
Factors that support reintegration:
- **Employment**: Stable job with living wage
- **Housing**: Safe, affordable, stable housing
- **Treatment**: Continued substance use and mental health treatment
- **Family**: Supportive relationships and family reunification
- **Community**: Social connections and prosocial networks
- **Services**: Access to healthcare, benefits, education

## Key Issues and Reforms

### Collateral Consequences Reform
- **Problem**: 40,000+ collateral consequences nationwide
- **Solutions**:
  - Collateral consequences databases
  - Automatic expungement/sealing
  - Ban the box policies
  - Fair chance licensing
  - Voting rights restoration
  - Housing discrimination protections

### Supervision Reform
- **Problem**: High revocation rates for technical violations
- **Solutions**:
  - Graduated sanctions
  - Incentive-based supervision
  - Reduced supervision terms
  - Evidence-based conditions
  - Earned discharge

### Employment Access
- **Problem**: Unemployment rates 5x higher for formerly incarcerated
- **Solutions**:
  - Ban the box
  - Fair chance hiring
  - Subsidized employment
  - Entrepreneurship support
  - Occupational licensing reform
  - Employer incentives

### Housing Stability
- **Problem**: Homelessness rates 10x higher for formerly incarcerated
- **Solutions**:
  - Public housing ban elimination
  - Landlord education
  - Housing vouchers
  - Transitional housing
  - Permanent supportive housing

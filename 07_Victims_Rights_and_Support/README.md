## Victim Rights Framework

### Constitutional Rights
- Right to be reasonably protected
- Right to reasonable notice of proceedings
- Right to be present at proceedings
- Right to be heard at critical stages
- Right to confer with prosecutor
- Right to restitution
- Right to proceedings free from unreasonable delay
- Right to be treated with dignity and respect

### Digital Rights
- Right to control personal identity and likeness
- Right to remove non-consensual intimate imagery (NCII)
- Right to protection from deepfakes and synthetic media
- Right to evidence preservation and chain of custody
- Right to privacy in legal proceedings
